Language flag icons for the language switch button.

Current resource aliases:
- :/flags/us.xpm -> English
- :/flags/ru.xpm -> Russian

Physical files in this folder:
- us.xpm
- ru.xpm

How to use your own images:
1) Replace us.xpm and ru.xpm with your own XPM files (same names), or
2) Keep your files and change paths in MainWindow.cpp -> updateLanguageButtonVisuals().

Recommended size:
- 20x14 px (current UI)
- 24x16 px (also good)
